<p><i>Scrcpy for Android</i> is an Android port of <a href='https://github.com/Genymobile/scrcpy' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Scrcpy</a>. This application mirrors display and touch controls from a remote android device to android device.</p><p><i>Scrcpy for Android</i> uses ADB-Connect interface to connect to android device to be mirrored. The latter needs to have <b>ADB-connect/ADB-wireless/ADB over network</b> enabled in developer settings, then enter its IP in the app on the other device. Full instructions can be found in the app repository's Readme.</p>
